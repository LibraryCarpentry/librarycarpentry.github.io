By Chris Erdmann

Stickers are a great way to show your support for, and celebrate your affiliation to Library Carpentry. We get frequent requests for Library Carpentry stickers but haven't been able to avail them at every event. Thanks to the LIR HEAnet User Group for Libraries we will have plenty of stickers on hand for the attendees of our LIBER-LIR-Library Carpentry instructor training 2-day event.

They were able to print the stickers from the images in our Library Carpentry Style Guide created by Drew Heles and David Kane:

https://github.com/LibraryCarpentry/lc-styleguide

You too can print the stickers out using a local/online service, but if you would like, we can also send you stickers. If you would like Library Carpentry stickers sent to you, please let us know by filling out this Library Carpentry stickers request form before 30 June 2019.

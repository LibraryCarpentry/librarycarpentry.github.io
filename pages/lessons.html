Our Core Curriculum

Our Core Curriculum lessons have been taught many times, and have been further refined based on instructor and learner feedback. This is designed for learners coming from a wide variety of library and information-related backgrounds. For learners with specific interests or needs (e.g., data analysis or archives), we recommend a number of themed lesson pathways below.

The Length of lessons offered below is an estimated range. The actual time to deliver a lesson can vary widely depending on how many exercies are offered, the backgrounds of learners, and more.

Lesson Pathways

The following Library Carpentry lessons can be taught instead of (or in addition to) our core curriculum. The pathways suggest combinations of lessons that comprise a full Library Carpentry workshop. You can also mix and match lessons in other ways, or teach standalone modules, though be sure to check prerequisites as some lessons require background knowledge.

Please consider the Carpentries lesson life-cycle status listed next to each lesson when considering whether or not to teach it. In general, Stable and Beta lessons are more robust than Alpha and Pre-Alpha lessons. Alpha and Pre-Alpha lessons not been taught or refined as often as lessons in our Core Curriculum.
